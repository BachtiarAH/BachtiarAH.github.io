Muarambaduk

1 Mar 2023 - current

I have had the opportunity to work on the development and design of a web and mobile application for Wisata Muarambaduk, a tourism destination. This application aimed to provide visitors with an interactive and informative experience. My responsibilities included implementing various features, such as ticket purchasing and camping equipment rental, to enhance the user journey. I ensured that the application included comprehensive information about Muarambaduk's tourist attractions, facilities, and locations, allowing visitors to plan their trips effectively. By enabling online ticket purchases and convenient equipment rentals, I contributed to streamlining the visitor experience and improving the efficiency of these processes. The ultimate goal of the project was to offer a more comfortable and practical travel experience for all users.
